My research lies at the intersection of moral psychology and philosophy of art. I am interested in how our emotional lives reveal human vulnerability, imperfection, and value.

## Dissertation Project

My dissertation explores the dark sides of emotional life, including negative emotions in art, weakness in love, and creativity as resistance against oneself.

## Areas of Interest

- Philosophy of Emotion
- Moral Psychology
- Philosophy of Art
- Aesthetics
- Love and Vulnerability
- Creativity
